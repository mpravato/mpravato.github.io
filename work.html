Summary

Asking me to focus on one thing just isn't possible. It allows me to balance serious reporting with comical cultural analyses. I tell the stories of student artists and explain Jersey Shore's impact on my generation all in the same week. I continue to make an important impact on the DMV arts community and serve as a simple source of laughter for my audience.

I am continuing to explore the world of social media marketing too. I had full creative control over two social media accounts for different University of Maryland academic departments. I was challenged to put forward professional content that was still attractive to college students and appropriate for the platforms. I redesigned their unattractive academic messages by planning, shooting, and editing my own photos/graphics/videos. I even created a few memes along the way. I rapidly grew both accounts' audiences and engagement rates by appealing to their bases more effectively.
